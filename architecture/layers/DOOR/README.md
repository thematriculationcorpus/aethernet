# DOOR

## Role
DOOR is the controlled business-to-business interface layer.
It enables limited, structured interaction between internal systems and external entities.

## Position in Architecture
DOOR sits downstream of VAULT and upstream of WINDOW.
It mediates exposure without revealing private internals.

## Inputs
- Authorized requests from external entities
- Structured offerings approved by governance
- Compliance-aligned interaction signals

## Outputs
- Limited responses
- Structured exchanges
- Controlled disclosures

## Non-Responsibilities
- No execution
- No persuasion
- No narrative framing
- No interpretation
- No automation
- No exposure of VAULT contents
