# REVOCATION RULES — Structural Canon

Defines how authority, permissions, or continuity claims are revoked.
No execution. No behavior. No automation.

## Revocation Triggers
- Boundary violation
- Authority escalation attempt
- Drift detection
- Integrity failure

## Revocation Scope
- Agents
- Runtime permissions
- Autonomy thresholds
- Continuity claims

## Effects
- Immediate invalidation
- Reversion to safe baseline
- No self-reinstatement

## Authority
Human override is final.
